About Study Buddy Finder

Study Buddy Finder is a platform designed to help students connect with like-minded study partners. Whether you're struggling with a difficult subject, preparing for an exam, or just looking for someone to study with, this app is the perfect solution!

Features

User-friendly profiles to list subjects and study preferences.
Find and connect with study partners based on interests and availability.
Real-time chat system for seamless communication.
Customizable profiles to highlight study goals and progress.
